About Us

We are a group of students (Carissa, Zoey, Darrian, and Nuel) who created this website to share and explain important physics concepts. Our goal is to present the topics in a simple and clear way so that students can understand the ideas more easily.

On this website, we explore several physics topics including Kinematics, Thermal Energy, Static Electricity, and Electromagnetic Induction. Each section explains the main concepts, formulas, and examples to help learners understand how these physics principles work and how they appear in real life.
